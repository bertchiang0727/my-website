About Me

I am a Master's student in Mechanical Engineering at UC San Diego with a strong passion for robotics, mechatronics, and intelligent systems. My research focuses on uniting mechanical design, embedded systems, and intelligent control to advance robotic platforms that tackle real-world problems. With over four years of leadership experience in international competitions and technical project management, I have cultivated both engineering expertise and team coordination skills. I am proficient in Python, C/C++, MATLAB, ROS, YOLO, CAD/PCB design (Inventor, Autodesk, Fusion360, EasyEDA), and rapid prototyping techniques such as CNC machining, 3D printing, and laser cutting. My long-term goal is to advance in the fields of biotech and medical robotics, leveraging interdisciplinary knowledge to design innovative solutions that improve healthcare and human well-being.
